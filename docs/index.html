NUvention Web 2017

Social Livestreaming Analytics

Our mission is to help publishers/video content creators make sense of live video analytics by aggregating and giving insights into audience engagement. Armed with that information, we hope they can predict successful opportunities for relevant livestreaming.

Our Customers

We’re focusing on customers who are savvy with social media tools: Brand ambassadors and social media personalities, social media managers, and video producers whose responsibilities include social livestreaming. This is includes Facebook Live, Instagram Live, Periscope, Twitch, and YouTube Live.

Building a product that meets your needs

Are you a regular livestreamer, whether personally or professionally? What’s the most important insight for you to know - before and after - when going live? Livestreaming is a young medium for social media, and we want to build an insightful tool as it grows.
