NUvention Web 2017

Team D, representing the media and publishing space for the 2017 NUVention Web and Media program

Facebook Live Periscope YouTube Live Twitch Meerkat (RIP) These are the major players in what’s very much the wild West of social livestreaming. While the analytics for social media are more mature, focusing on engagement, comments, impression, it’s less clear what the key performance indicators for livestream video are. It’s our goal to make sense of it for not only social media managers, but brand ambassadors and, ultimately, anyone with a smartphone
